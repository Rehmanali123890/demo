About Us

At Yellow Power General Maintenance, established in 2023, we are committed to advancing infrastructure projects in Abu Dhabi and across the UAE. Our expertise includes road work, building maintenance, electrical installations, and general infrastructure projects. We pride ourselves on delivering high-quality services to both the public and private sectors.

We handle projects ranging from street lighting installations to kerbstone and interlock tile placements, as well as structure backfilling and blockwork construction. Every project is managed with utmost precision, ensuring the highest safety standards and professionalism.
