Yellow Power General Maintenance is a leading service provider specializing in road work, building maintenance, electrical installation, and general infrastructure projects. Based in Abu Dhabi, we take pride in our decade-long experience, providing comprehensive maintenance services to both public and private sectors.

Our scope of work includes street lighting installations, kerbstone and interlock tile placement, and blockwork construction for buildings. We ensure that every project is handled with the utmost precision and quality, from earthworks and backfilling to the installation of lighting poles and related infrastructure.

With a dedicated and skilled team, we have successfully completed numerous projects with key clients, including ADNOC Service Stations and the Sweihan Park and Cycle Track. Our commitment to safety, professionalism, and excellence sets us apart as trusted partners in the maintenance industry.

Why Choose Us?

At Yellow Power, we specialize in various road and building maintenance services, including street lighting, kerbstone installation, interlock tiling, and building block work. Our expertise is built on years of successful project completion in Abu Dhabi and beyond.

From start to completion, we manage every aspect of the project, including earthwork cutting, filling, structure backfilling, and the installation of street lighting poles with foundation and cable laying.

Our team is highly trained and experienced in working on complex infrastructure projects. We ensure safety and efficiency in every step of the process, delivering excellent results for both road and building maintenance.

We have completed numerous projects with prestigious clients, such as ADNOC Service Stations and Sweihan Park and Cycle Track, ensuring the highest standards in every project we undertake.
