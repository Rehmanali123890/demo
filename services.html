Yellow Power Yellow Power General Maintenance provides a comprehensive range of services, specializing in road work, building maintenance, and electrical installations. We take pride in offering top-quality infrastructure services to both public and private sectors across the UAE.

Our services include street lighting installations, kerbstone and interlock tile placement, and building blockwork construction. From earthworks and backfilling to the installation of lighting poles and infrastructure, each project is handled with the utmost precision and attention to detail.

We have successfully delivered key services to prominent clients, including ADNOC Service Stations and Sweihan Park. Our commitment to safety, professionalism, and excellence ensures we exceed client expectations and contribute to the sustainable development of infrastructure in the UAE.
